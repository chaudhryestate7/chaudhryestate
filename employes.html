Our CEO

Chaudhry Shahzad.

Chaudhry Shahzad leads Chaudhry Estate and Builders with over 11 years of real estate expertise, driven by a passion for delivering exceptional results and building lasting relationships. With extensive knowledge of the market, particularly in Clifton, DHA, and top housing societies like Ary Laguna, ASF, DHA City, and Bahria Town, he expertly guides clients through every step of their real estate journey. As CEO, Chaudhry Shahzad oversees a dedicated team providing personalized service and expert advice for buying, selling, and investing in properties. His commitment to integrity, excellence, and client satisfaction sets him apart. He is dedicated to making clients' real estate aspirations a reality and helping them achieve their goals in the ever-evolving world of real estate.

Our Managing Director

Chaudhry Shahbaz.

Chaudhry Shahbaz is the visionary Managing Director of Chaudhry Estate, a leading real estate agency in Clifton, Karachi. With 9 years of expertise in property investment, he has established himself as a trusted authority in the field. His specialization in high-growth societies, including ARY Laguna, ASF, DHA City, and Bahria Town, has enabled him to drive the company's growth and success. Through his strategic leadership and commitment to client satisfaction, Chaudhry Shahbaz has led his team to execute numerous high-profile transactions, earning a reputation for excellence in the market. His expertise spans both residential and commercial real estate, making him a sought-after advisor for informed investment decisions. Under his guidance, Chaudhry Estate continues to thrive, setting new standards in the real estate industry.
